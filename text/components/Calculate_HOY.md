## ![](../../images/icons/Calculate_HOY.png) Calculate HOY

![](../../images/components/Calculate_HOY.png)

Calculate hour of the year. -

#### Inputs
* ##### month [Default]
Month (1-12).
* ##### day [Default]
Day (1-31).
* ##### hour [Default]
Hour (0-23).
* ##### minute [Default]
Minute (0-59).

#### Outputs
* ##### hoy
Hour of the year.
* ##### doy
Day of the year.
* ##### date
Human readable date.
